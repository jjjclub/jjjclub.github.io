Our Story

JJJ was created during the COVID-19 Pandemic. During the lockdown at the time, the club founders formed the JJJ with a small group of friends. The main goal was to try to stay connected even at long distances or differing schedules.

As time moved forward, some friends of friends decided to join. Our passion for videogames have become the bridge in which we bond. Today, we strive to extend and facilitate this bond to other people. We are doing so by developing an easy and effortless way of communicating (via discord) and storing our memories (via our website).

Fun Fact! The three founding members noticed they all had J's in their real name. Thus, the JJJ Club was born.
